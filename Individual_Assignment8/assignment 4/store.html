About Vinyl Haven

Welcome to Vinyl Haven, your premier destination for vinyl records in Melody City. Established in 2013, we pride ourselves on offering a curated selection of new and used vinyl records spanning multiple genres including rock, jazz, blues, and indie.

Our knowledgeable staff is passionate about music and always ready to help you find that perfect album. We host regular listening parties and live performances from local artists, creating a vibrant community for music lovers.

Special Collection: Check out our rare vinyl section featuring limited editions and signed copies!
